Mission - Teachers

1. To motivate teachers to be role models to their students.
2. To empower teachers to add value to themselves to consequently improve upon their teaching craft.
3. To instill into teachers the joy of the profession.
4. To research into better teaching methods and how to make education work better.
5. To instill into teachers an increased level of achievement motivation

Mission - Students

Inspire students especially those in less endowed schools/areas through:
1. Organization of student motivational sessions
2. Mentorship
3. Volunteerism
4. Extra-curricular activities

Vision

To impact education by empowering teachers and students in our communities to live to their highest potentials.
